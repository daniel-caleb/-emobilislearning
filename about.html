Mission

The mission of eMobilis is to create opportunities for African youth by training them on digital, software and other technologies that prepare them for the future of work by equipping them with marketable, industry driven skills. Our goal is to ensure that our talented youth are available and trained in order to meet the growing opportunities and needs of the ICT Industry in Africa. We also envision an explosion of technology innovation and the creation of successful tech ventures by local developers from across the country with a zeal for changing the world through harnessing the power of their technology training for social good.

Vision

Our vision is to empower local youth to tap into the myriad opportunities that the mobile, technology and software development industry offers so that they can innovate, create and improve their situation in life through use of digital tools.
